## ⚠️ IMPORTANT — Before posting

1. **Verify hashtags** — Indian Instagram caps at 30 hashtags. Don't go over.
2. **Tag locations** — tag major cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore) on city-specific posts for local discovery
3. **Pin Post 1 of Day 1** as a profile-pin — it's your launch announcement
4. **Reply to every early comment** in the first 60 min to boost engagement
5. **Cross-post the Reels** to Facebook (free reach boost)
